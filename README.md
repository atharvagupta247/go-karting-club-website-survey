This repository contains a curated survey of Indian go-karting clubs, college teams, national competitions, and professional karting organisations

The purpose of this research is to study **landing pages and hero sections** to help design a **modern, high-impact go-karting club website** using technologies like **React, Next.js, and Three.js**.

---

Objective of This Repository

- Research existing go-karting related websites in India
- Analyze how clubs and teams present themselves online
- Study hero sections, CTAs, visuals, and layout
- Identify gaps and opportunities for a modern landing page
- Use insights to design a new go-karting club website

 What Was Observed During the Survey

- Most student club websites are **content-heavy but visually outdated**
- Hero sections lack **strong headlines and CTAs**
- Minimal use of animations or interactivity
- Almost no use of **modern frontend frameworks**
- Huge scope for **React / Next.js / Three.js based redesign**

---

Design Direction Inspired By This Research

Based on the survey, a modern go-karting club landing page should include:
- A bold hero section with strong typography
- High-quality racing visuals or 3D elements
- Clear Call-to-Action (Join / Register / View Team)
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Mobile-first responsive design
